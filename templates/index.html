MY PROJECTS

Karibu Inventory System

Python Django SQLite

Before Karibu Inventory System, businesses struggled with manual record-keeping, misplaced stock, and frequent errors in sales and inventory tracking. Staff spent hours reconciling stock, and managers lacked real-time insights, leading to lost sales and overstocking.

Karibu solved these problems by automating inventory management, providing real-time stock updates, and generating instant sales reports. Product categorization and low stock alerts help prevent shortages, while the intuitive dashboard empowers staff to manage products efficiently. As a result, businesses save time, reduce errors, and make smarter decisions.

  • Real-time stock tracking
  • Product categorization
  • Low stock alerts
  • Sales reporting
